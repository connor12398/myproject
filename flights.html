Flights and Planning

All flights must be planned ahead with our company by calling the serrvice number or e-mail. Flights can be made to and from the dstination. Flight time may vary depending on the chosen airline. It is recommended that vistors pre-plan their tours with an advisor before the flight of their arrival. Flight costs will be covered seperately from the tourism costs. When a customer selects a tour to a city to visit, they may pick one destination, two, or all three together. The triple city visit to Moscow, Saint Petersburg, and Vladivostok is by far the most popular.

Flight Offers

Customers can check their airline of choice to see if the airline has a partnership with us. If you fly on an airline partnered with Russian Tours, you can receive the the following offers.

  • Guidebooks on Russian art and history in the storage compartment of your plane seat.
  • Food menus with authentic Russian cuisine
  • A built in music player in the plane seat with many selections of Russian music, as well as almost every genre in other languages. Radio stations are also available.
  • A movie player with many films from Russia, as well as films from other countries
  • Extra storage for luggage
